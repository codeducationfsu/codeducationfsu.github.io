About Codeducation

Who We Are!

Codeducation is a student-led organization that thrives off the motto; Collaborate, Create, Discover. We are a platform for students to explore/discover their passion for technology. We also provide room for students to grow professionally and help build their careers.

Our goals are to equip members with knowledge, promote innovative thinking, and establish leaders campus-wide.
